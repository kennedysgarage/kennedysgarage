Project Description

StartupBus is an exclusive community of the best hackers, hustlers, & designers. Once a year in Americas and Europe, they run an annual competition that thousands apply for but only the best are selected, to board a bus and three days later have conceived, built and launch a startup 1800 miles from the departure point.
