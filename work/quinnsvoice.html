Quinn's Voice, the voice for everything including radio spots, audio guides, phone systems, audiobooks, TV, movies, and more.

Logo

The man behind Quinn's Voice is Kevin Quinn. I first heard that voice of an angle when he ran a morning show on at a radio station. Once I started sketching out and playing around with this brand I knew exactly what I wanted to do.

It had to be in the shape of a coffee mug. Supper smooth with bold lines. I figured that others could relate to this and the idea is endless. I played around with adding a "V" into it, but it just didn't work out. Sometimes less is more.

I create this little animation to help paint a better picture of what I had in mind with it.
