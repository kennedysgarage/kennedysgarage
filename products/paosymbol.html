When I was building Beach Hair I needed to include a PAO Symbol on the label. I looked around for a while and had no luck finding a vector version of it. So I did want any creative would do. I created it and then shared it with the public to use. I also included the vector on the Wikipedia page about to future other others.

Website

Every website starts with a domain, so I purchased paosymbol.com. The website is dead simple, I wanted to let people know what the site was for and the direct link to download it.
