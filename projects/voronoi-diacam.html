I hacked on Voronoi Diacam at the Photo Hack Day. We built a Voronoi Diagram over google maps that uses geolocation and a timestamp to place an image from Flickr & FourSquare into cells to fill up Manhattan. I won the food eating contest and the team won best use of Flickr's API.

We have decided to share this project on GitHub: https://github.com/kennedysgarage/Voronoi-Diacam
