Let's take a look back

I was on the 2011 StartupBus with Team Lemonade Stand. We crafted a neighborly mobile commerce platform that enables people to buy and sell goods within a community. We opened the pathway for such companies such as Zaarly and the dozen others that pop up after us. We had over 30 articles written up about us that eventually lead us to be techcrunched. All the hard worked help the team reach the semifinals, finishing in the top 12 of 38 teams. When we got back home we were honored with Hack of the Month at NY Tech Meetup. Keep in mind that we only had 48 hours to build our product and everything else that goes with the territory, crazy how much a dedicated team can complete in such a short time.
