Mr. Kennedy was a website that built to keep friends and family up-to-date while my kid brother was having a liver transplant.

Website

The first website I create was made within a few hours after sitting at the hospital. I was getting a lot of text, phone call, Facebook messages, etc. I figure I would bring all communication to one place. A few years later I redesigned the site to be more of a timeline to remember.
